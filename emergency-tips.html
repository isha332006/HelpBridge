Quick Tips for Emergencies

  • Create an Emergency Plan: Discuss evacuation routes and meeting points with your family.
  • Stay Informed: Monitor local news or weather updates for warnings and alerts.
  • Emergency Kit: Always have a kit with essentials such as water, food, flashlight, batteries, and first aid supplies.
  • Avoid Panic: Stay calm and act quickly but carefully in emergencies.
  • Communication: Keep your phone charged and carry a power bank for backup.
  • ID & Documents: Keep copies of important documents in a waterproof bag.
  • Avoid Hazards: Stay away from downed power lines, damaged buildings, or floodwaters.

Lifesaving Actions During Specific Disasters

- **Fire:** Stop, drop, and roll if your clothes catch fire. - **Flood:** Move to higher ground immediately. - **Earthquake:** Duck under sturdy furniture.
