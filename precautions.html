General Safety Guidelines

  • Stay calm and follow instructions from authorities.
  • Keep an emergency kit ready with essentials like water, food, flashlight, and first aid supplies.
  • Know your local evacuation routes and shelters.
  • Avoid using elevators during earthquakes or fires.
  • Turn off gas, electricity, and water supplies if instructed to do so.

Specific Precautions

Floods:

Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters. Move to higher ground immediately.

Earthquakes:

Duck under sturdy furniture and hold on until the shaking stops. Stay away from windows and heavy objects.

Cyclones:

Secure loose items outdoors and stay indoors in a safe room.

Fires:

Crawl low under smoke and use a wet cloth to cover your nose and mouth.
